What is Bionic Reading ??

Bionic ReadingÂ® revises texts in such a way that the most concise parts of the words are highlighted. This guides the eye over the text and the brain remembers words already learned more quickly. Bionic reading is a technique that uses cognitive processing to help you understand and recall information more deeply. It helps break down words and sentences into their component parts and guides your eyes to them, so you can see how each component fits together and better understand the overall message.

THE COLLABORATION

IT students and Education students are working together to see if bionic reading, which uses technology to help people read, is actually helpful. They're testing it out in real classrooms and with different types of learners to understand how well it works and if it makes learning easier for everyone. By combining their skills, they hope to figure out if bionic reading can truly make education better and more accessible for all.
